Engineer

Designer

  • Interaction Design

    While I was a student at the visual interaction design lab, I took several courses on this topic and have learnt to appreciate principals of good interaction design. At Jimu lab I had a unique opportunity to put my interaction design knowledge into practice.

  • User Experience

    I have conducted and participated in several user studies involving data collection techniques such as video and audio recording, questionnaires and field notes.     During my graduate studies I have often used paper and video prototyping tools to communicate my design ideas by creating low fidelity prototypes. I am also comfortable using Inkscape and gimp for prototyping

  • Information Visualization

    Information Visualization is another topic that I explored during my school years. I have created small interactive visualization using D3.js, jsPlumb and a few other libraries. I also created static visualizations of data using the R environment and tools on Linux.
